# Cairn — AI Habit Coach for ADHD

> Working name. Final name confirmed before App Store submission (week 7).

A neuroaffirming habit and focus companion designed specifically for adults with ADHD. Shame-free streaks, AI body-doubling, time-blindness aids, one-tap capture.

**Status:** Week 3 — onboarding, settings, accessibility, and polish in progress.

## Why this app, why now

- ADHD adults are an underserved $1B+ subset of the productivity market (r/ADHD: 1.9M, TikTok #adhd: 30B+ views).
- Mainstream habit apps actively harm ADHD users with rigid streaks and shame loops.
- AI as a kind, adaptive coach is genuinely new — not a chatbot bolted onto a tracker.
- iOS-first: ADHD adults skew higher-income; iOS users pay 3-4× more than Android in this category.
